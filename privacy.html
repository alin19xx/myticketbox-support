Privacy Policy

Support

Effective Date: January 31, 2026

MyTicketBox is designed to work without our own servers. Your ticket data is stored locally on your device. Optical Character Recognition (OCR) is processed on-device using Apple’s Vision framework. We do not sell your data and we do not use third-party advertising trackers.

1. Data Stored by the App (On Device)

For each ticket, the app may store:

  • Ticket ID (UUID)
  • Amount
  • Date
  • Category
  • Store name (optional)
  • Note (optional)
  • Ticket image (optional, stored locally)
  • Creation date

Storage: Local database on your device (SwiftData). No external server sync is used.

2. System Permissions

  • Camera: to capture ticket photos
  • Photos: to import ticket images from your photo library

These permissions are used only when you explicitly choose to capture or import an image. Images are stored locally on your device.

3. OCR (On-Device Processing)

MyTicketBox uses Apple’s Vision framework to recognize text on your device. The recognized text may be processed temporarily to prefill fields (amount, date, store name, and category). The full raw recognized text is not stored permanently. You can always edit fields before saving.

4. In-App Purchases (Subscriptions)

Subscriptions are handled by Apple StoreKit. We do not receive your payment information. Product identifiers:

  • com.myticketbox.premium.monthly
  • com.myticketbox.premium.yearly

5. PDF Export & Sharing

When you export, MyTicketBox generates a PDF locally on your device (temporary system directory). The PDF may include selected ticket data and images, plus a summary (totals and category breakdown). The app does not upload PDFs to our servers.

If you choose to share a PDF, it is shared using iOS system sharing, and the destination depends on what you select.

6. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or contact list data is collected by the app.
  • No location data is collected.
  • No third-party advertising tracking is used.
  • No analytics data is collected by default.

7. Data Deletion

  • You can delete individual tickets inside the app.
  • Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data from your device.
  • Purchase history is managed by Apple and can be restored via your Apple ID.
